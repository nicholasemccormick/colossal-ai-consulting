Section 01

Introduction

Welcome to Colossal AI Consulting ("App," "we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information about you when you use our application, which integrates with the LinkedIn platform via LinkedIn's official APIs.

By connecting your LinkedIn account or otherwise using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please disconnect your account and discontinue use.

This application is not endorsed by, affiliated with, or officially connected to LinkedIn Corporation or its subsidiaries. Use of LinkedIn data is governed by LinkedIn's User Agreement and Privacy Policy.
Section 02

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account registration details (name, email address, password)
  • Profile information you choose to enter within the App
  • Support requests, feedback, and communications

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Log data including pages visited, timestamps, and referring URLs
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions within the App

LinkedIn API Data

With your explicit authorization, we may access certain LinkedIn data as described in Section 3 below.

Section 03

LinkedIn Data Access

Our App requests only the minimum LinkedIn permissions required to deliver its core functionality. The scopes we request may include:

  • r_liteprofile — Basic profile information (name, profile picture, headline)
  • r_emailaddress — Your primary email address for account creation
  • w_member_social — Ability to post, comment, or react on your behalf (only when you initiate an action)
  • r_organization_social — Read access to organization pages you manage (if applicable)
We will never: access LinkedIn data beyond what you authorize, store your LinkedIn credentials, sell or transfer your LinkedIn data to third-party advertisers, or use your data to re-identify you across other services.

LinkedIn data accessed through the API is used solely for the purpose for which it was granted. We comply with LinkedIn's API Terms of Use at all times.

Section 04

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the App and its features
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your session
  • Perform actions on LinkedIn that you explicitly request
  • Send transactional communications (account alerts, security notices)
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues
  • Monitor for fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your data for targeted advertising, nor do we build advertising profiles based on your LinkedIn activity.

Section 05

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

Trusted vendors who assist in operating our infrastructure (hosting, analytics, email delivery) under strict confidentiality agreements and data processing terms.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or the public.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Section 06

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide the App's services or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your active account.
  • LinkedIn API data is cached only as long as needed to fulfill your request and is purged within 30 days unless you request longer storage for specific features.
  • Log data is retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes.

Upon account deletion, we will remove or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.

Section 07

Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards to protect your data, including:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • OAuth 2.0 token-based authentication (we never store your LinkedIn password)
  • Periodic security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting data to authorized personnel only

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at security@colossalai.consulting.

Section 08

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection / Restriction — Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Withdraw Consent — Revoke LinkedIn API access at any time via your LinkedIn account settings under Settings & Privacy → Data Privacy → Permitted Services

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@colossalai.consulting. We will respond within 30 days.

Section 09

Cookies & Tracking

We use a minimal set of cookies essential to the App's operation:

  • Session cookies — Keep you logged in during a browsing session
  • CSRF tokens — Protect against cross-site request forgery attacks
  • Preference cookies — Remember your display and language settings

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the App may not function correctly as a result.

Section 10

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete it.

Continued use of the App after changes are effective constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
